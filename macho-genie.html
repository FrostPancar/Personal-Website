01

Overview

Macho Genie is a men’s essentials brand selling premium sports boxers and briefs in a wide range of colorways. I designed a web and email UI system that could streamline Shopify updates, weekly campaign production, and handoff to developers while keeping the brand recognizable and expressive.

Scope
Design System, UI, Product
Role
System Design
02

The challenge

The challenge was to bring structure to a fast-moving founder-led brand without flattening what made it appealing. Macho Genie needed clearer rules for merchandising, campaigns, and handoff, but it also had to preserve a tone that felt premium, playful, and confidently flamboyant rather than generic men’s basics.

03

Building the foundation

The foundation started with the basics the team could reuse every week: naming patterns, spacing logic, typographic hierarchy, layout rules, and modular structures for both site and email. The goal was to reduce production drag while giving designers and developers a common system to build from.

04

Tokens and visual rules

Tokens were used as both implementation tools and brand controls. Type, color, spacing, radius, pricing treatments, promotional accents, and CTA rules were defined so the system could stay recognizable across Shopify surfaces and email modules, even as campaigns and product stories changed week to week.

05

Core components

Core components were built around the patterns the brand used most often: product cards, promotional blocks, collection modules, PDP elements, buttons, and reusable email sections. This made the system practical for real merchandising work instead of just serving as visual documentation.

06

Applying the system

Once the system was in place, it could be applied across web updates and weekly newsletter production with much less friction. That made launches cleaner, merchandising decisions faster, and campaign execution more consistent across product pages, promo surfaces, and email sends.

07

Outcome

The result was a more usable operating system for the brand: faster design cycles, cleaner launches, stronger recognizability, and an easier handoff process for developers. More importantly, the system gave Macho Genie a way to scale its web and email output without losing the premium, playful identity that made the brand distinct.

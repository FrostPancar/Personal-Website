01

Project overview

Sitch NYC needed a sharper public face for a fast-moving drinks brand: something that could live equally well on the web, in launch assets, and across social content without losing its edge.

02

Challenge

The system had to feel immediate and culturally tuned, but also stay organized enough to scale across campaign drops, product storytelling, and quick-turn marketing needs.

03

Creative direction

The direction focused on a clean but charged visual language: bold contrast, editorial typography, and a flexible framing system that could hold photography, copy, and product equally well.

04

Web design

The site system translated the brand logic into a modular interface, balancing strong type with modular content blocks that could flex between campaigns, launches, and evergreen storytelling.

05

Social toolkit

A simple toolkit of templates, crops, type rules, and pacing devices made it possible to produce a broad range of social assets while keeping the output coherent across channels.

06

Rollout across channels

Once the foundation was established, the system could extend across launch collateral, email, campaign messaging, and platform-specific touchpoints without needing to be reinvented each time.

07

Outcome

The resulting system gave Sitch NYC a stronger brand presence and a more consistent toolkit for launch moments, everyday updates, and future growth across the brand ecosystem.
